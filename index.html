Personalized nutrition made simple

NutriTrack empowers users to log meals, scan barcodes, and receive AI-driven nutrition advice. The client needed an app that felt both scientific and approachable.

Our Approach

We built a Flutter app integrated with Google’s ML Kit for barcode recognition and a custom nutrition API. The UI uses calming blues and clean typography to reduce cognitive load during meal logging.

TaskPulse

Team productivity, reimagined

← Back to Projects

TaskPulse helps remote teams stay aligned with voice notes, priority tagging, and real-time task updates. The client wanted a native experience on both iOS and Android.

Execution

We developed parallel native apps (Swift for iOS, Kotlin for Android) with a shared WebSocket backend. Voice notes are transcribed in real time using on-device speech recognition for privacy.
